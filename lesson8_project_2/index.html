Cost of Self-driving cars

Self-driving cars comprise of expensive materials, one of which is lidar, the material that gives the cars a 3-D sensor. Lidar for one self-driving car can cost a whopping $75,000. This means that coming up with a self-driving car made with a cost-effective budget may present difficulties in the coming years.
